Interchange Rate
PSD2 regulated flat cap — used for recovered transaction value only
▼
Under PSD2, interchange is capped at 0.20% (consumer debit) and 0.30% (consumer credit) regardless of authentication method. There is no differential between frictionless and challenged transactions. This rate is used only to calculate the interchange income earned on transactions recovered from challenge screen abandonment.
Portfolio Card Type
Interchange Rate 0.25%
Methodology: Under PSD2 / UK SCA, all in-scope CNP transactions are authenticated (ECI 05/02) — there is no non-authenticated baseline. This model compares step-up challenge (current state) vs Outseer TRA-exempt frictionless (target). Both paths produce ECI 05/02; the issuer bears liability on both. Fraud detection rate saving = reduction in missed fraud cases × loss per missed transaction. OTP/SMS cost applies to the OTP portion of challenges only. Abandonment saving uses a blended rate weighted across OTP and OOB channels. Manual review rate is derived from confirmed fraud case volumes. Interchange rate applies only to interchange income on recovered abandoned transactions — no differential exists under PSD2 for consumer cards.
